Evos Solutions - Who we are?

Established in 2006, we are a specialized and energetic team offering technical solutions and services across diverse industries. Innovation is at the core of our approach towards solving and simplifying business challenges of our partners. Based out of Bhubaneswar, our organization primarily focuses on software product development which range from stand alone systems to large fully integrated ERP software solutions. Our team has rich and diverse experience in fields like product management, project management, internet technologies, web technologies which assures we deliver high quality work in a timely manner. We specialise in the fields of data mining and analytics, big data, machine learning, web development, website optimisation, SEO, UI/UX, custom CRM software. We have experience in executing projects as partners as well managing green field projects. We also develop on-demand IoT solutions using state of the art technology available.We have an industry expertise in a wide range of sectors, energy and power, education, health, agriculture businesses, government to name a few.
