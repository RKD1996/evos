About Us

Evos Solutions provides end-to-end business solutions that leverage technology. We are really focused on creating a virtuous cycle. We continuously invest in providing wonderful customer experience to allow us to make investments in technology and operations to further improve the customer experience. For us designing software isn't like adding a coat of paint rather we make well-built software thinking about users and their need. We engrain our years of experience of user behavior analysis and human interaction into every project. The team size is dynamic and can be scaled up at a short notice as we have a time-tested system in place to be flexible and sensitive towards client's needs which help them deliver their business goals and drive technology led transformations.
